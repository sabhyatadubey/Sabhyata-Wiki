# Metric Definitions

## Email Open Rate
**Definition:** Percentage of delivered emails that were opened by the recipient.
**Current benchmark:** — (no data in source yet)
**How it's calculated:** Unique opens / Delivered emails × 100

---

## Email CTR (Click-Through Rate)
**Definition:** Percentage of delivered emails where the recipient clicked at least one link.
**Current benchmark:** —
**How it's calculated:** Unique clicks / Delivered emails × 100

---

## WhatsApp CTR
**Definition:** Percentage of delivered WhatsApp messages where the recipient clicked the deep link.
**Current benchmark:** —
**How it's calculated:** Unique clicks / Delivered messages × 100

---

## Push CTR
**Definition:** Percentage of push notifications delivered where the recipient tapped through.
**Current benchmark:** —
**How it's calculated:** Taps / Delivered push notifications × 100

---

## Campaign Conversion Rate
**Definition:** Percentage of campaign recipients who completed a booking after receiving the campaign.
**Current benchmark:** —
**How it's calculated:** Bookings attributed to campaign / Recipients × 100

---

## Reactivation Rate
**Definition:** Percentage of lapsed customers (no booking in 60+ days) who make a booking after a re-engagement campaign.
**Current benchmark:** —
**How it's calculated:** Lapsed customers who booked / Lapsed customers contacted × 100

---

## Voucher Redemption Rate
**Definition:** Percentage of voucher codes sent in campaigns that were actually used at checkout.
**Current benchmark:** —
**How it's calculated:** Vouchers redeemed / Vouchers sent × 100

---

## Retention Rate
**Definition:** Percentage of customers who make a repeat booking within 90 days of their previous booking.
**Current benchmark:** —
**How it's calculated:** Customers with repeat booking in 90 days / Total active customers × 100

---

## Churn Rate
**Definition:** Percentage of active customers who have not made a booking in 60+ days.
**Current benchmark:** —
**How it's calculated:** Customers with no booking in 60 days / Active customer base × 100

---

## Repeat Booking Rate
**Definition:** Percentage of total bookings that come from returning customers.
**Current benchmark:** —
**How it's calculated:** Bookings from returning customers / Total bookings × 100

---

## NPS (Net Promoter Score)
**Definition:** Customer satisfaction score from post-service surveys. Scale -100 to +100.
**Current benchmark:** —
**How it's calculated:** % Promoters (score 9–10) minus % Detractors (score 0–6)
