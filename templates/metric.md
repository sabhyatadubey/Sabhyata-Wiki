# Metric Definitions

## Retention Rate
**Definition:** Percentage of customers who make a repeat booking within 90 days of their first booking.
**Current benchmark:** —
**How it's calculated:** (Customers with 2+ bookings in 90 days) / (Total customers with first booking in period) × 100

---

## Churn Rate
**Definition:** Percentage of active customers who have not booked in 60+ days.
**Current benchmark:** —
**How it's calculated:** (Customers with no booking in 60 days) / (Active customer base) × 100

---

## Repeat Booking Rate
**Definition:** Percentage of completed bookings that come from returning customers.
**Current benchmark:** —
**How it's calculated:** (Bookings from returning customers) / (Total bookings) × 100

---

## Reactivation Rate
**Definition:** Percentage of lapsed customers (60+ days inactive) who make a booking after a re-engagement campaign.
**Current benchmark:** —
**How it's calculated:** (Lapsed customers who booked) / (Lapsed customers contacted) × 100

---

## Email Open Rate
**Definition:** Percentage of delivered emails that were opened.
**Current benchmark:** —
**How it's calculated:** (Unique opens) / (Delivered emails) × 100

---

## Email CTR
**Definition:** Percentage of delivered emails where the recipient clicked at least one link.
**Current benchmark:** —
**How it's calculated:** (Unique clicks) / (Delivered emails) × 100

---

## SMS CTR
**Definition:** Percentage of delivered SMS messages where the recipient clicked the link.
**Current benchmark:** —
**How it's calculated:** (Unique clicks) / (Delivered SMS) × 100

---

## Campaign Conversion Rate
**Definition:** Percentage of campaign recipients who completed a booking.
**Current benchmark:** —
**How it's calculated:** (Bookings attributed to campaign) / (Recipients) × 100

---

## CLV (Customer Lifetime Value)
**Definition:** Average total revenue generated by a customer over their lifetime with Justlife.
**Current benchmark:** —
**How it's calculated:** Average order value × Average booking frequency × Average customer lifespan

---

## NPS (Net Promoter Score)
**Definition:** Customer satisfaction score from post-service surveys. Scale of -100 to +100.
**Current benchmark:** —
**How it's calculated:** % Promoters (9-10) minus % Detractors (0-6)

---

## Loyalty Redemption Rate
**Definition:** Percentage of loyalty rewards issued that are redeemed by customers.
**Current benchmark:** —
**How it's calculated:** (Rewards redeemed) / (Rewards issued) × 100
